## 🔥 Common tricky questions + sagot

### ❓ “Bakit bubble sort hindi ibang algorithm?”

👉

> Because it is simple to implement and suitable for demonstrating sorting logic, especially for small datasets.

---

### ❓ “Ano time complexity?”

👉

> The time complexity is O(n²) in the worst and average case.

---

### ❓ “Ano mangyayari kung maraming data?”

👉

> It becomes inefficient because bubble sort is not optimal for large datasets.
